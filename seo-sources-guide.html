03 — The 80/20 Breakdown
The 20% that drives 80% of learning
  • Growth Memo every Sunday — this is your strategy layer. One read per week, 15 minutes, keeps you ahead of where SEO is going (not just where it's been)
  • Ahrefs keyword research posts — one deep read per month on their methodology gives you a repeatable system you can use forever
  • Any Ahrefs data study — when they publish original research (like "AI Overviews cut clicks by 34.5%") that's must-read. It informs your business decisions, not just your content
  • Google Search Console — not a reading source, but after month 4 it becomes your most valuable teacher. Real data from your real site beats any blog post
The 80% to consciously skip
  • SEL daily news — it updates constantly. Reading it daily is a trap. You don't need to know every algorithm tweak. A Friday 5-minute skim is enough
  • SEO Twitter/X rabbit holes — full of contradictory opinions, self-promotion, and drama. Ahrefs and Kevin filter the signal from that noise for you
  • Technical SEO deep dives (crawl budgets, log file analysis, etc.) — not your layer yet. This is for when you have 500+ pages and enterprise clients
  • Tools comparison posts — Ahrefs vs Semrush vs Moz debates. You don't need a paid SEO tool right now. GSC + free Ahrefs webmaster tools is enough to start
