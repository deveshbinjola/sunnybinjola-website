The 5-Phase Healing Framework

01

Understand What's Happening to You

Your brain processes heartbreak through the same neural pathways as physical pain. The numbness, the obsession, the inability to think — it's neuroscience, not weakness.

02

Regulate Your Nervous System

Cold exposure, breathwork, and movement — the biological tools that reset a system stuck in fight, flight, freeze, or fawn. Your body heals before your mind does.

03

Feel What You've Been Avoiding

Men who suppress grief show increased rates of depression, anxiety, and addiction. Somatic practices and witnessing are how you process without drowning.

04

Rebuild Your Identity

You didn't just lose her — you lost the version of yourself built around her. Mirror work, pattern recognition, and brotherhood help you rebuild from the inside out.

05

Step Into Post-Traumatic Growth

Post-traumatic growth after heartbreak is documented and real — but it doesn't happen by accident. It happens when you build a structure that holds the grief long enough for it to transform you.
