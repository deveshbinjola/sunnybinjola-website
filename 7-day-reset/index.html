What Happens Over 7 Days

Each day builds on the last. By day 7, you'll have walked from collapse to purpose.

01

The Collapse

When the identity breaks before the heart. Why heartbreak feels like death — and your first breathwork practice to pull you out of survival mode. 3 minutes. That's all.

02

No Contact

You can't heal from a story you're still rehearsing. Why 90 days of space works, how to say it with love, and what happens when you stop feeding the old bond.

03

The Letter You Don't Send

What stays unsaid stays stuck. Write everything — the anger, the regret, the missing, the accountability. Then don't send it. This gets the story out of your body.

04

The Mirror

Partners are mirrors. You don't lose her — you lose yourself trying to keep her. Radical self-accountability for where you stopped leading your own life.

05

The Rebuild

You don't get strong alone. Brotherhood, daily practice, movement, breath, writing — the foundation of a masculine path. Not bitter. Better.

06

Who Am I Without Her?

Your identity isn't broken — it's been separated. The parts that depended on her are collapsing. The parts that were always yours are still here. Today you learn the difference.

07

Purpose

Pain is the strongest energy stored in your body. It can become anger and rage — or it can become purpose. This series was designed for transmutation. Day 7 is where you choose your path forward.
