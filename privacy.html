Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

Your privacy matters. This policy explains how Sunny Binjola Coaching ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit sunnybinjola.com or use our services.

Information We Collect

Information you provide

When you sign up for our 7-Day Reset, book a discovery call, join a coaching program, or contact us, you may provide:

  • Name and email address
  • Phone number (if provided for coaching sessions)
  • Any information you share during coaching conversations
  • Payment information (processed securely by third-party providers; we do not store payment details)

Information collected automatically

When you visit our site, we may automatically collect:

  • Browser type and device information
  • Pages visited and time spent on site
  • Referring website or search terms
  • IP address (anonymized where possible)

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Deliver coaching services and programs you've signed up for
  • Send you the 7-Day Reset email sequence and related content
  • Respond to your questions and discovery call bookings
  • Improve our website and services
  • Send occasional updates about new programs or content (you can unsubscribe anytime)

Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to run our business. These include:

  • Beehiiv — for email delivery and newsletter management
  • Supabase — for secure data storage
  • Cal.com — for scheduling discovery calls
  • Cloudflare — for website hosting and security

Each of these services has its own privacy policy. We only share the minimum information necessary for them to provide their service.

Cookies

Our site uses minimal cookies for essential functionality (such as remembering your preferences). We do not use advertising cookies or sell your data to advertisers.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Coaching Confidentiality

Everything shared in coaching sessions is treated as confidential. We will never share the content of your sessions with anyone without your explicit written consent, except where required by law (e.g., if there is an immediate risk of harm).

Children's Privacy

Our services are designed for adults (18+). We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact
