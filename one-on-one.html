Questions?

What does a session actually look like?

expand_more

Sessions are 60-90 minutes via video call. We start with a check-in about what's happened since we last spoke. Then we move into the work — that might be somatic practice (breathwork, grounding), dialogue coaching, mirror work, or whatever serves your transformation. You leave with practices to integrate before the next session.

How long do people typically work together?

expand_more

Every man's journey is unique. Most start with 3-6 months of weekly work to get grounded, then continue longer if the momentum is real. Some men work with me for a year or more as they move through different layers of transformation. We talk about what feels right for you during the discovery call.

What's your approach?

expand_more

I blend somatic coaching with transformational dialogue. We work with your body, your nervous system, your story. This isn't about thinking your way out of your patterns — it's about feeling your way through them, so you can actually live differently. Rooted in Vedic wisdom and modern embodiment practices.

What if I'm not sure I'm ready?

expand_more

Book the discovery call anyway. We'll talk about where you are and what's true for you right now. If 1:1 coaching isn't the right fit yet, I might suggest a group program or the Heal Your Heartbreak course first. My job is to help you find the right container for your transformation, not to convince you to work with me.
